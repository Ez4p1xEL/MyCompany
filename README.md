## About MyCompany

## Description
MyCompany is a Bukkit plugin that introduces a company management system in Minecraft servers.
Players can found company, playing as an employer, and hiring other players to be the employee of the company.
Each member of the company needs to earn income through **daily employee orders**, **chest shops** and **company area trade**.
Each company needs to pay daily salaries and handle taxes, in order to create realistic economic gameplay.

MyCompany needs your ideas and feedback to grow and reach the goal of immersive economic gameplay
